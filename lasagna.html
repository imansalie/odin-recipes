Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of rich, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey mozzarella cheese. It's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

prepared with fresh ingredients and seasoned to perfection, this recipe will surely be a hit at your table. Serve it with a side salad and some garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a bit of Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

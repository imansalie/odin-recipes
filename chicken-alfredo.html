Chicken Alfredo

Description

This Chicken Alfredo is a creamy and comforting pasta dish made with tender chicken, a rich Alfredo sauce, and fettuccine. It's a classic favorite.

Great for a satisfying dinner, this dish is easy to prepare and perfect for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Chicken breasts
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season and cook the chicken breasts in a pan until fully cooked. Slice into strips.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and cook garlic until fragrant. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine and chicken to the sauce, tossing to coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.

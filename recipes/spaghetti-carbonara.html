Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This Spaghetti Carbonara is a rich and creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's simple to make yet full of flavor.

Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this dish will satisfy your pasta cravings.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss the hot spaghetti with the pancetta, then quickly mix in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve immediately.
